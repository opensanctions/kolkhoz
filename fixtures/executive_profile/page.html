Helix Biotech

Chief Scientific Officer: Søren Bjørk

Søren Bjørk has served as Chief Scientific Officer of Helix Biotech since 2019. A molecular biologist by training, he previously held research posts at the University of Copenhagen and the Karolinska Institute before joining the company.

Under his direction, Helix Biotech has expanded its pipeline of RNA therapeutics and opened a new research facility in Boston.

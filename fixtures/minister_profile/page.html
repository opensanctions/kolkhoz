Helena Marchetti, Minister for Education

Helena Marchetti is the current Minister for Education for the State of Victoria.

About the Minister

Name:
Helena Marchetti
Date of birth:
12 March 1971
Country:
Australia
Biography:
A former schoolteacher and trade union organizer who entered parliament in 2006.

The Role

Position:
Minister for Education
Jurisdiction:
the State of Victoria
Description:
The cabinet minister responsible for state schools, vocational training, and the education budget.
Took office:
1 February 2018
Term expires:
31 December 2027

Personal life

Outside politics, Marchetti is married to novelist Andrea Lombardi; the couple lives in Melbourne.
